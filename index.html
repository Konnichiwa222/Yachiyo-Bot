🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Yochiyo Hub only collects data necessary for its features to function. By using the bot, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

  • Guild / Channel / Role IDs — for server configuration (prefixes, anti-link, autorole).
  • User IDs — for tickets, giveaways, streaks, and AFK status.
  • Message content — scanned in real-time for anti-link and autoresponder; never stored permanently.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords or credentials
  • Personal information (real names, emails, addresses)
  • Private / Direct Messages
  • Voice data

4. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to operate bot features. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any third-party purpose.

5. Data Storage & Sharing

Data is stored locally in JSON files on our server. We do not share data with any third party. The only external service used is Discord's official API.

6. Data Deletion

Server admins can clear stored data at any time. You may also contact the Yochiyo team via Discord to request full deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years of age, in line with Discord's own requirements.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact the Yochiyo team directly via Discord.
